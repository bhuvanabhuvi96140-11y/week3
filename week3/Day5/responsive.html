Responsive Percentage Width Demo

This demo uses percentage widths and basic media queries. Resize or open on mobile to test.

Sidebar

Sidebar — typically used for navigation, filters, or secondary information. In this demo it takes 25% of the row on wide screens and stacks to full width on small screens.

Main

Main — primary content area. Here it occupies 50% width on wide screens so it remains visually dominant while the sidebar and extras flank it.

Extras

Extras — supplemental content such as related links, ads or widgets. At 25% it balances the sidebar on wide screens and moves below on narrow screens.

One third

One‑third column — equal-width column (33.33%) when used as a three-column layout. Good for cards, feature lists or small pieces of content.

One third

One‑third column — repeats to show how equal columns behave at different viewport sizes.

One third

One‑third column — on mobile these stack vertically for readable, single-column flow.
